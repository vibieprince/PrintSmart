Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for my prints?
We support all major UPI apps including GPay, PhonePe, and Paytm. Payment is secure and instant.
Can I choose color or black & white?
Yes! You have full control over print settings including Color/BW, Single/Double sided, and Paper Size (A4/A3).
What if I miss my pickup time?
Your prints are kept safe at the shop. While we recommend picking up on time to avoid crowds, you can collect it later in the day using your token.
